## Purpose

This part treats creative as an engineering discipline — not a subjective one. The question is not "is this a good ad?" but "does this ad produce the signals the system needs to learn, allocate, and scale?"

> [!rule] Creative Engineering Framing
> Every creative decision is a signal decision. You are not designing for human aesthetics; you are designing for the machine's embedding and prediction systems, while also being compelling enough to generate the human behaviors those systems measure.

## Key Creative Rules in This Part

> You do not scale ads. You scale **portfolios.**
> Never vary randomly. Use a **variation matrix.**
> Creative identity is latent — it emerges from signals, not intent.
